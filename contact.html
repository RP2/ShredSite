Aaron Stapley

Creative Director

Aaron has spent two decades designing and marketing consumer goods. Prior to forming Stapley-Hildebrand in 2004, he served as Creative Director for Primo Angeli, Inc. where he led design programs for a wide range of clients including: Adobe Systems, Inc., The Coca Cola Company, Heineken USA, and Allied Domecq Spirit and Wine USA. He began his career as a Designer and Design Manager for Néstle USA.

Aaron has won numerous design industry awards and his work has been featured in Graphis, How Magazine, Graphic Design USA, and The New York Times.
